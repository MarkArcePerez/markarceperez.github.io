About Me

I’m an analytics professional passionate about modernizing legacy systems and building scalable reporting solutions that support better decision-making. Currently, I’m a Health Business Analyst at Florida Blue, where I lead the end-to-end management of three KPI reporting programs for value-based healthcare initiatives. As the team’s R specialist, I’ve automated and validated reporting pipelines, significantly reducing manual work while improving speed, accuracy, and reliability. My work also includes migrating SAS-based processes into R/SQL frameworks, refining attribution logic, and developing interactive R Shiny dashboards to make reporting more accessible and actionable. In addition to my healthcare work, I serve as an Instructional Associate for Georgia Tech’s Applied Analytics Practicum and as an Analytics Consultant for a nonprofit supporting at-risk families in Afghanistan. Across each role, my focus is the same: building strong data systems, improving workflows, and using analytics to create measurable impact.
